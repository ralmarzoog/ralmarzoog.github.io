About Me

Born and raised in Dhahran, Saudi Arabia, I moved to the suburbs of Portland, Oregon when I was 16. After finishing my last two years of high school in Oregon, I moved to Massachusetts to study at Tufts University. I'm now a rising senior majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Colonialism Studies.

My interests outside of programming and colonialism studies include drawing, reading, eating more chocolate than seems humanly possible, and binge-watching TV shows.
